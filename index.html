Scientific Laboratory of Biogerontology

This database has been developed as part of the State Assignment (Госзадание) for the current research period.

Our mission is to systematize data on natural extracts with potential geroprotective properties. We focus on molecular mechanisms such as:

  • Senolytic activity (clearance of senescent cells)
  • Autophagy induction
  • Sirtuin activation and mTOR signaling modulation

All data is synchronized with PubMed and verified by our research team. This digital tool is intended for internal laboratory use and expert analysis.

Note: This is an open-access resource for scientific collaboration.
